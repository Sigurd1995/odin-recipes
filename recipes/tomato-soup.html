Tomato soup

Description

Tomato soup is a plain and simple, but savory dish.

It´s delicious on its own — just add an egg or some pasta to make it even more satisfying.

Ingredients

  • Canned tomatoes
  • Tomato paste
  • Vegetable broth
  • Navy beans
  • Fresh garlic cloves
  • Onion powder
  • White miso
  • Coconut sugar
  • Dried basil

Steps

  1. Place all ingredients in a large, high-speed blender (with an 8-cup capacity or more). Blend for 30 seconds or until smooth.
  2. Carefully transfer soup to a large stockpot. Heat over medium until slightly boiling.
  3. Then reduce heat, cover, and gently simmer for about 15 minutes, stirring occasionally.
  4. Add salt and black pepper to taste if desired. Serve plain or with fresh basil, vegan parmesan cheese, black pepper, etc.
