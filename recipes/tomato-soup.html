Tomato soup

Description

Tomato soup is a plain and simple, but savory dish.

It´s delicious on its own — just add an egg or some pasta to make it even more satisfying.

Ingredients

  • Canned tomatoes
  • Tomato paste
  • Vegetable broth
  • Navy beans
  • Fresh garlic cloves
  • Onion powder
  • White miso
  • Coconut sugar
  • Dried basil
