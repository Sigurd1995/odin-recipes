Blueberry jam

Description

Blueberry jam is a great condiment and fits perfectly with pancakes.

Combine it with crispy bacon for a perfect balance of sweet and salty!

Ingredients

  • Fresh Blueberries
  • Granulated White Sugar
  • Lemon Juice
  • Cardamom

Steps

  1. Prepare the Blueberries – Wash the berries, pulling out any stems or bad berries. Pulse blueberries in a blender to roughly chop.
  2. Cook the Jam – Add berries, sugar, lemon juice, and cardamom to a wide, shallow pan. Bring to a boil over medium heat. As mixture boils down, it will foam up and reduce. Stir occasionally to ensure it’s not sticking. If it does stick, reduce the heat.
  3. Thicken the Jam – Once the foam begins to subside, start to stirring constantly. Jam will darken in color and take on a thick, syrupy consistency. It’s done when it coats the back of a spoon and you can draw a line down the center of the pan that takes a moment to refill.
  4. Cool the Jam – Allow the blueberry jam to cool 5 – 10 minutes in the pan, just enough to make it safe to handle. Transfer the hot jam to a clean glass jar or storage container. Cool the jam at room temperature for about 90 minutes before refrigerating. Chill 6 – 8 hours to allow it to set and thicken completely.
